Why I chose programming

I've always loved tech. When I wasn't biking around with my friends as a kid I was either playing video games or opening up / messing with my desktop computer. I broke it a few times but always managed to fix it again. Later on I ended up going into art as a professional interest, a family tradition, but I continued to play around with computers and learn applications like Photoshop and Illustrator. In college I took a basic web design class and fell in love with the more hands-on computer work. I slowly inched my way into more coding, eventually trying out basic JavaScript. After some success, I felt confident this was what I wanted to do as a job. Not wanting to go back to school again, I learned about Epicodus and felt like that was the perfect way to break into the tech field.
